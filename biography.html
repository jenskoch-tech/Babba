Peter Koch

Born 1946, Wrescherode, Germany

Peter Koch was born in the spring of 1946 in a small town in the Harz Mountains in the North of Germany. The third oldest of four children ..... The family moved to Frankfurt for better opportunities in 19...?

He attended the local Freiherr vom Stein school, where he was greatly inspired by one of his teachers who had travelled extensively in South America. Peter would buy books on emigration other continents early on.

Peter got married at a young age and to support his family joined the German Air Force in 196?? Basic Training way a place smells different on a Sunday morning.

He was married to Suzanne for over fourty years and had two children, each of whom has at some point been told a story at bedtime that went on rather longer than planned. He remains unapologetic about this.

He began writing his stories down in earnest after retirement, encouraged by his family to stop keeping them to himself. What follows on this site is the result — a collection assembled over several years of quiet mornings, strong tea, and the particular pleasure of getting things right.
