Welcome

This small corner of the internet is a place where family, old friends, and curious readers can sit down with a cup of coffee and enjoy the stories Peter has gathered over a lifetime.

Born post-war Germany, Peter learned early that the best entertainment came from the imagination. Raised in the city of Frankfurt and early on exposed to other cultures he was shaped by decades of adventure, travel, and quiet observation, he turned to writing in his later years to preserve and share the moments that mattered most — and a few that simply made him laugh.

The stories here range from childhood memories to living abroad, from gentle comedy to moments of reflection. They are written for pleasure — his, and hopefully yours.

Browse at your leisure. Leave a message in the Guestbook if the spirit moves you. And above all, enjoy.
